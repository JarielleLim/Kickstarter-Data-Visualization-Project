Success expectancy for
Kickstarter Projects in Canada

By Jarielle Lim & Joshua Sun

A data visualization of Kickstarter projects in Canada between 2014 to 2018,
focusing on success and failure rates in the project type categories

Interactions:

  • select bar charts to create learn more information about the specific category
  • hover over bars/points for tooptip information
  • select legend to show and hide bar
  • click buttons to sort/split chart
  • hover over success/failed for clearer readings in second visualization
